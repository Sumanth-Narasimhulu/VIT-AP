Super Dream Offer

In this placement offer, the students are presented with offers with Cost of Company (CTC) of 10 Lakhs and above per annum. Last year, about 140 companies offered 1176 super dream placements offers. Among them, Microsoft has offered the highest CTC of 41.60 lakhs per annum. 7 students have got their placement in Microsoft with the Super Dream Offer. This year, we achieved more than double the previous year record on Super dream offers and stand at 2938 super dream offers.
